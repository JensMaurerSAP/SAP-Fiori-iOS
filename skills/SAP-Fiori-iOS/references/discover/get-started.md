## What is SAP Fiori for Apple OS?

SAP Fiori for Apple OS is SAP's design language adapted specifically for iOS and iPadOS. It combines SAP's enterprise design system with Apple's native platform conventions, bringing together the Horizon visual theme, SAP 72 typography, and iOS Human Interface guidelines into a cohesive experience.
