# SAP Design System — Discover

> SAP Fiori for iOS | Discover

## What is it?

The SAP Design System provides a consistent and holistic user experience for SAP software across all devices. It combines visually pleasing design with a strong focus on ease of use — intuitive, simple, and powerful across the enterprise.

---

## Five Core Design Principles

### Role-Based
The design system is built for your specific business role, needs, and the way you actually work. It draws from broad insights into the multifaceted roles of today's workforce — delivering the right information at the right time.

### Delightful
Beyond productivity, the SAP Design System enriches your work experience. It lets you simply do your job without friction.

### Coherent
Whether fulfilling a sales order, reviewing KPIs, or managing leave requests — the same consistent interaction and visual design language applies across the entire enterprise.

### Simple
Complete your work intuitively and quickly. Essential functions are easy to use. The experience can be personalized to focus on the tasks that matter to you.

### Adaptive
Work how and where you want, regardless of device. The design system provides relevant information that allows for instant insight on any screen size or platform.

---

## How These Principles Apply to SAP Fiori for iOS

| Principle | iOS/iPadOS expression |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Role-Based | Contextually relevant overview pages, personalized KPI headers, facet-based navigation |
| Delightful | Fiori animations, SAP 72 typography, Joule AI copilot, smooth transitions |
| Coherent | Shared Horizon color tokens, consistent component behavior across platforms |
| Simple | One-tap interactions, progressive disclosure, clear information hierarchy |
| Adaptive | Size class system, NavigationSplitView, `.sidebarAdaptable`, compact/regular layouts |
