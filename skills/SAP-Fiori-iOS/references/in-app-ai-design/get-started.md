# In-App AI — Get Started

> SAP Fiori for iOS | In-App AI Design

## What is it?

SAP embeds generative AI into mobile business apps through two approaches:

1. **In-App AI components and patterns** — contextual, embedded AI that enhances specific workflows
2. **Joule** — SAP's conversational AI copilot for open-ended, cross-module tasks

---

## Two AI Approaches

### In-App AI (Embedded)
AI integrated directly into the app's UI. Delivers real-time recommendations, predictive analytics, and task automation within the user's natural workflow without interruption.

**Best for:** Contextual, specific, fast actions within the current screen.

### Joule (Conversational Copilot)
Conversational AI panel providing contextual insights across SAP solutions and third-party data. Handles complex, multi-step, or cross-module tasks using natural language.

**Best for:** Complex queries, bulk actions, cross-module tasks, exploration, and guidance.

---

## Design Principles

- **Relevant** — AI surfaces information at the right moment in the workflow
- **Reliable** — users can review and validate AI-generated content before acting
- **Responsible** — transparent about what AI is doing; users stay in control
